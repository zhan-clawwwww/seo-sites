Disney+ offers region-specific content libraries, but accessing them outside your country—or watching the US library while traveling—requires a VPN that can bypass Disney+'s geo-blocks. Error code 73 ("not available in your region") appears when Disney+ detects VPN use, so you need a provider that stays ahead of detection.

## How Disney+ VPN Detection Works

Disney+ checks IP addresses, DNS resolvers, and sometimes device location. VPNs that work consistently use modern protocols (WireGuard, Lightway), frequently refreshed IP pools, and built-in DNS leak protection. Smart DNS or SmartPlay features help on devices like Smart TVs or Fire Stick where full VPN apps aren't available.

## Top VPNs for Disney+ 2026

**NordVPN** — Best overall. NordLynx protocol delivers sustained 4K streaming, and SmartPlay DNS reduces routing errors. Large server network with frequent refreshes keeps Disney+ access reliable.

**ExpressVPN** — Best for the Disney+/Hulu/ESPN+ bundle. MediaStreamer works on devices without VPN apps. Strong performance for Netflix, Disney+, Hulu, and BBC iPlayer.

**Surfshark** — Best value. Unlimited simultaneous connections suit families. Budget-friendly with solid streaming performance.

## Disney+ Bundle Deals in 2026

The Disney Bundle includes Disney+, Hulu, and ESPN+ (exact tier depends on plan). Pricing ranges from about $9.99 to $24.99 per month—cheaper than subscribing separately. Bundle availability is US-only; VPN plus a US payment method is required for non-US residents.

## Setup Tips

1. Choose a VPN with US servers and streaming-optimized options
2. Enable DNS leak protection before connecting
3. Use a server labeled for streaming if available
4. On Smart TVs or game consoles, configure Smart DNS or MediaStreamer per provider instructions

If you see error 73, try another US server or wait for your provider to refresh its IP pool.

## VPN Features That Matter for Disney+

Effective Disney+ VPNs share several traits: modern protocols (WireGuard or Lightway) for 4K bandwidth, residential-looking IP pools with frequent refreshes, and DNS/IPv6 leak protection. Smart DNS or SmartPlay features are essential for devices without native VPN apps—many users stream Disney+ on Smart TVs, Apple TV, or Fire Stick. Ensure your VPN's DNS doesn't leak; otherwise Disney+ will detect your real location and block access.

## Pricing and Value

NordVPN and Surfshark often run discounts on longer plans—up to 70% off on 2-year subscriptions. ExpressVPN costs more but offers MediaStreamer and consistently fast speeds. Consider how many devices you'll use: Surfshark's unlimited connections suit large households, while ExpressVPN's 14-device limit covers most families.
