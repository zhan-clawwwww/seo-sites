## Overview

The MacBook Air (13") from 2017 represented Apple's vision for portable computing at the time. Whether targeting professionals or everyday users, it offered a refined balance of performance, portability, and build quality that defined the MacBook lineup.

This model continued the tradition of combining powerful internals with a sleek, durable chassis. Users could expect reliable performance for productivity tasks, creative work, and everyday computing. The MacBook Air lineup has always prioritized the balance between power and portability. From coding and video editing to web browsing and document work, this machine was built to handle diverse workloads.

## Key Specifications

The MacBook Air 13" delivered the following hardware:

- **Display**: 13.3" LED (1440×900)
- **Processor**: Intel Core i5/i7 (Kaby Lake)
- **Storage**: 128-256GB SSD
- **Memory**: 8GB DDR3
- **Battery**: 54 Wh

These specifications positioned the device competitively within its segment at the time of release. The combination of hardware choices reflected Apple's focus on delivering a balanced, user-centric experience rather than chasing raw numbers.

The MacBook Air (13") from 2017 delivered solid performance for its era. Its display and processor were well-suited to the workflows of the day.
