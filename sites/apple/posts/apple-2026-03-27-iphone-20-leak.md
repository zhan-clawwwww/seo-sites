Leaked CAD files and renders from Apple's supply chain reveal the most dramatic iPhone redesign since the original iPhone. The iPhone 20, codenamed "Nova," will feature a revolutionary foldable design and unprecedented AI integration.

## Foldable Revolution

The iPhone 20 will mark Apple's first foray into foldable smartphones:

- **7.8-inch Foldable Display**: Unfolds to tablet size, folds to pocketable 5.8-inch
- **Ceramic Shield Pro**: 5x more durable than current glass
- **Hinge-less Design**: Seamless folding with no visible crease
- **Dynamic UI**: Adapts layout based on folded/unfolded state

## AI-Powered Camera System

The camera represents Apple's biggest leap in computational photography:

- **Quad 48MP Sensors**: Each with AI-specific processing capabilities
- **Neural Imaging Engine**: Real-time scene analysis and enhancement
- **Proactive Photography**: AI suggests optimal shots before you take them
- **3D Holographic Capture**: Creates interactive 3D models of subjects

## Performance and AI Integration

Powered by the A20 Bionic chip with dedicated AI cores:

- **AI Performance**: 50x faster than current A18 chip for AI tasks
- **On-Device LLM**: Full local language model processing
- **Predictive Intelligence**: Learns user patterns to anticipate needs
- **Privacy-First AI**: All processing happens on-device

## Revolutionary Features

The iPhone 20 introduces several groundbreaking technologies:

1. **Haptic Keyboard**: Virtual keyboard with physical feedback
2. **Solar Charging**: 30% daily charge from ambient light
3. **Health Monitoring**: Continuous blood glucose and blood pressure monitoring
4. **Spatial Audio Pro**: Personalized sound based on ear shape
