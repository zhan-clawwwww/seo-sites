## Overview

The iPhone Air launched in September 2025 as Apple's thinnest iPhone ever at just 5.64mm. Despite its slim profile, it packed a 6.5-inch Super Retina XDR display with ProMotion, the A19 Pro chip, and a capable 48MP Fusion main camera with 2x telephoto. Weighing only 165g, the iPhone Air offered a unique blend of portability and performance for users who prioritized a lightweight, elegant design.

The iPhone Air targeted customers seeking a premium experience in a remarkably thin form factor. With up to 1TB storage and full Apple Intelligence support, it delivered a complete flagship experience while redefining what was possible in smartphone thinness.

## Key Specifications

The iPhone Air delivered the following hardware:

- **Display**: 6.5-inch Super Retina XDR OLED (2736×1260, 460 ppi), ProMotion up to 120Hz
- **Processor**: Apple A19 Pro (5-core GPU variant)
- **Storage**: Up to 1TB
- **Camera**: 48MP Fusion main with 2x telephoto, digital zoom up to 10x
- **Design**: 5.64mm thickness, 165g weight

These specifications positioned the iPhone Air as a distinct option in the lineup—premium performance in the slimmest possible package. The single 48MP camera with 2x capability provided strong image quality without the bulk of a multi-camera array.

## Design

At 5.64mm, the iPhone Air was Apple's thinnest iPhone ever. The aluminum unibody construction ensured durability despite the slim profile. IP68 water and dust resistance, Ceramic Shield 2, and a refined aesthetic made it both beautiful and practical. The lightweight 165g build made one-handed use comfortable for extended periods.

## Conclusion

The iPhone Air remains a standout in the 2025 lineup for its unprecedented thinness and lightweight design. Its combination of A19 Pro performance, ProMotion display, and capable 48MP camera delivered a premium experience in a form factor that felt like the future. For users who valued portability and elegance, the iPhone Air was an compelling choice.
