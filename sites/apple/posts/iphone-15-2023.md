## Overview

The iPhone 15 marked another step in Apple's smartphone evolution when it launched in 2023. Building on the foundation of its predecessors, it delivered meaningful improvements in performance, display quality, and camera capabilities. The device quickly became a popular choice for users seeking a balance of features and value.

Apple has consistently refined the iPhone formula with each generation, and the iPhone 15 was no exception. From the display to the chipset, every component was chosen to deliver a cohesive user experience. The iPhone 15 found its place among consumers who wanted a reliable, well-supported device with access to the full iOS ecosystem. Whether for photography, productivity, or entertainment, the iPhone 15 offered a versatile platform that adapted to a wide range of user needs.

## Key Specifications

The iPhone 15 delivered the following hardware:

- **Display**: 6.1-inch Super Retina XDR OLED (2556×1179, 460 ppi)
- **Processor**: Apple A16 Bionic
- **Storage**: 128GB, 256GB, 512GB
- **Camera**: 48MP main + 12MP ultra wide, 12MP front
- **Battery**: 3349 mAh

These specifications positioned the device competitively within its segment at the time of release. The combination of hardware choices reflected Apple's focus on delivering a balanced, user-centric experience rather than chasing raw numbers.
