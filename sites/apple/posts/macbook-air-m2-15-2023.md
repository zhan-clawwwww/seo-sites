The MacBook Air M2 15" delivered the following hardware:

- **Display**: 15.3" Liquid Retina (2880×1864)
- **Processor**: Apple M2
- **Storage**: 256GB-2TB SSD
- **Memory**: 8-24GB unified
- **Battery**: 66.5 Wh
