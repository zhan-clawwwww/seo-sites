The iPhone 14 Pro Max delivered the following hardware:

- **Processor**: Apple A16 Bionic
- **Storage**: 128GB, 256GB, 512GB, 1TB
- **Camera**: 48MP main + 12MP ultra wide + 12MP telephoto, 12MP front
- **Battery**: 4323 mAh
