## Overview

The iPhone X marked another step in Apple's smartphone evolution when it launched in 2017. Building on the foundation of its predecessors, it delivered meaningful improvements in performance, display quality, and camera capabilities. The device quickly became a popular choice for users seeking a balance of features and value.

Apple has consistently refined the iPhone formula with each generation, and the iPhone X was no exception. From the display to the chipset, every component was chosen to deliver a cohesive user experience. The iPhone X found its place among consumers who wanted a reliable, well-supported device with access to the full iOS ecosystem. Whether for photography, productivity, or entertainment, the iPhone X offered a versatile platform that adapted to a wide range of user needs.

## Key Specifications

The iPhone X delivered the following hardware:

- **Display**: 5.8-inch Super Retina OLED (2436×1125, 458 ppi)
- **Processor**: Apple A11 Bionic
- **Storage**: 64GB, 256GB
- **Camera**: Dual 12MP (wide + telephoto), 7MP TrueDepth front
- **Battery**: 2716 mAh

These specifications positioned the device competitively within its segment at the time of release. The combination of hardware choices reflected Apple's focus on delivering a balanced, user-centric experience rather than chasing raw numbers.

## Design

Edge-to-edge OLED, Face ID, notch. The build quality reflected Apple's attention to detail, with materials and finishes that felt premium in hand. Ergonomics were carefully considered to ensure comfortable one-handed use where applicable. Weight distribution and button placement contributed to an intuitive, satisfying user experience.

## Conclusion

The iPhone X remains a notable entry in iPhone history. Its combination of display quality and processing power made it a compelling option at release. For collectors or those on a budget, it can still handle basic tasks like messaging, browsing, and light apps, though software support has likely ended for older models. If you're considering a used iPhone X, battery health and storage capacity are the main factors to evaluate.
