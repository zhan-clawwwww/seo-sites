## Overview

The iPhone 17 Pro Max launched in September 2025 as the largest and most capable iPhone. It featured a 6.9-inch Super Retina XDR display with ProMotion, the A19 Pro chip, and the same 48MP Pro Fusion camera system as the Pro—with dual telephoto (4x and 8x) for up to 16x optical zoom. With up to 2TB storage, it offered the most capacity in the iPhone lineup for professionals and content creators.

Weighing 233g, the iPhone 17 Pro Max delivered the best battery life and largest viewable area in the 2025 lineup. Ceramic Shield 2, Dynamic Island, and IP68 rating ensured durability for demanding use.

## Key Specifications

The iPhone 17 Pro Max delivered the following hardware:

- **Display**: 6.9-inch Super Retina XDR OLED (2868×1320, 460 ppi), ProMotion up to 120Hz, 3000 nits peak outdoor brightness
- **Processor**: Apple A19 Pro (6-core CPU, 6-core GPU, 16-core Neural Engine)
- **Storage**: Up to 2TB
- **Camera**: 48MP Pro Fusion (main 24mm, ultra-wide 13mm, 4x telephoto 100mm, 8x telephoto 200mm), up to 16x optical zoom
- **Weight**: 233g

These specifications positioned the iPhone 17 Pro Max as the ultimate iPhone for users who wanted the largest display, best battery life, and maximum storage without compromising on camera or performance.

## Design

Aluminum unibody with Ceramic Shield 2, Dynamic Island, and IP68 water and dust resistance. The 6.9-inch display provided an immersive canvas for content consumption, gaming, and creative work. Despite its size, the 233g weight and balanced distribution made it manageable for extended use. The build quality reflected Apple's commitment to premium materials and finishes.

## Conclusion

The iPhone 17 Pro Max remains the flagship choice for users who want the best of everything. Its 6.9-inch display, 48MP Pro Fusion camera with dual telephoto, A19 Pro performance, and up to 2TB storage delivered an unmatched combination. With the best battery life in the lineup and full Apple Intelligence support, it will serve power users for years to come.
