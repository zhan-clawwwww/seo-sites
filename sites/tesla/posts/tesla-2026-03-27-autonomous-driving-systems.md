# Autonomous Driving Systems

Autonomous driving combines sensors, AI, and control systems to enable vehicles to navigate without human input.

## Sensor Technologies

Cameras, radar, and lidar provide complementary perception. Sensor fusion combines data for robust environmental understanding.

## AI Algorithms

Deep learning enables object detection and prediction. Planning algorithms determine safe vehicle trajectories.

## Safety Architecture

Redundant systems and fail-safe mechanisms ensure safety. Validation requires extensive testing across diverse scenarios.

## Regulatory Landscape

Standards and regulations vary by region. Liability frameworks continue to evolve as technology matures.
