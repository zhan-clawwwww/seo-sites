## Fundamental Investment Strategies

### 1. Long-Term Holding (HODL)

#### Core Principles
- **Time Horizon**: 5+ years minimum
- **Asset Selection**: Fundamental strength and network effects
- **Dollar-Cost Averaging**: Regular purchases regardless of price
- **Cold Storage**: Majority of assets in hardware wallets

#### Suitable Assets
- **Bitcoin**: Digital gold thesis, store of value
- **Ethereum**: Platform for decentralized applications
- **Blue-Chip Layer-1s**: Established networks with proven track records
- **Infrastructure Tokens**: Critical protocol components

#### Risk Management
- **Position Sizing**: Never more than 5% in any single asset
- **Rebalancing**: Quarterly review and adjustment
- **Exit Strategy**: Clear criteria for selling (fundamental breakdown, regulatory changes)
- **Insurance**: Third-party custody insurance where available

### 2. Active Trading Strategies

#### Day Trading
- **Time Frame**: Minutes to hours
- **Tools Required**: Real-time data feeds, advanced charting, algorithmic execution
- **Risk/Return**: High risk, potentially high returns
- **Suitable For**: Experienced traders with technical analysis skills

#### Swing Trading
- **Time Frame**: Days to weeks
- **Strategy**: Technical patterns and market momentum
- **Risk Management**: 2% maximum per trade, strict stop-losses
- **Tools**: Moving averages, RSI, MACD, volume analysis

#### Algorithmic Trading
- **Strategy Types**: Arbitrage, market making, trend following
- **Platforms**: Professional trading APIs and custom development

- **Risk Controls**: Circuit breakers, position limits, drawdown limits

- **Annual Returns**: 3-12% depending on network and validator selection
- **Risks**: Slashing penalties, validator downtime, centralization concerns
- **Platforms**: Native wallets, exchanges, dedicated staking services

- **Automated Market Makers**: Uniswap V4, Curve V3, Balancer V3

#### Lending and Borrowing

## Risk Management Framework

### 1. Position Sizing Strategies

#### Fixed Percentage Risk
- **Formula**: (Account Risk %) / (Stop Loss %) = Position Size %
- **Example**: 1% account risk with 10% stop loss = 10% position size
- **Advantages**: Consistent risk across trades
- **Disadvantages**: May limit position size in volatile markets

#### Kelly Criterion
- **Formula**: f* = (bp - q) / b
  - f* = fraction of capital to bet
  - b = odds received on bet (profit/loss ratio)
  - p = probability of winning
  - q = probability of losing (1-p)
- **Applications**: Optimizing bet size based on edge
- **Limitations**: Requires accurate probability estimates

#### Volatility-Adjusted Sizing
- **ATR-Based**: Position size inversely proportional to volatility
- **Beta Adjustment**: Considering correlation with broader market
- **Drawdown Control**: Reducing size during losing streaks
- **Scaling In/Out**: Gradual entry and exit from positions

#### Modern Portfolio Theory Adaptation
- **Asset Correlation**: Low correlation between crypto assets and traditional markets
- **Efficient Frontier**: Risk-return optimization for crypto portfolios
- **Rebalancing Frequency**: Quarterly or threshold-based (5% deviation)
- **Diversification Benefits**: Reduced volatility through uncorrelated assets

#### Core-Satellite Approach
- **Core Holdings (60%)**: Bitcoin, Ethereum, stablecoins
- **Satellite Positions (40%)**: Thematic bets, emerging projects, trading positions
- **Rebalancing Rules**: Trim winners, add to underperformers
- **Risk Budgeting**: Allocate risk based on conviction and timeframe

#### Thematic Allocation
- **Layer-1 Platforms**: Ethereum competitors with unique value propositions
- **DeFi Ecosystem**: Lending, trading, derivatives, insurance
- **NFT and Gaming**: Virtual assets and play-to-earn economies
- **Infrastructure**: Oracles, storage, computation, interoperability

### 3. Security Best Practices

#### Custody Solutions
- **Self-Custody**: Hardware wallets (Ledger, Trezor) with proper backup
- **Multi-Signature Wallets**: Required approvals from multiple keys
- **Institutional Custody**: Regulated custodians with insurance
- **Exchange Selection**: Reputable platforms with strong security track records

#### Operational Security
- **Private Key Management**: Never digital storage, physical backup only
- **Transaction Verification**: Double-check addresses before sending
- **Phishing Prevention**: Bookmark trusted sites, enable 2FA everywhere
- **Software Updates**: Regular updates for wallets and security software

#### Insurance Options
- **Custodial Insurance**: Coverage for assets held with third parties
- **Smart Contract Insurance**: Protection against code exploits
- **Personal Policies**: Home insurance riders for digital assets
- **DeFi Insurance Protocols**: Nexus Mutual, InsurAce, etc.
