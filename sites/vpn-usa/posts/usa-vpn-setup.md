This guide shows a simple **USA VPN setup** process for most VPN apps.

## Steps

1. Install the VPN app from the official website or app store.
2. Sign in and choose a **USA server location**.
3. Enable the **kill switch** (recommended).
4. Connect and confirm your IP/location changed.

## Verify it works

- Check IP location websites
- Run a DNS leak test
- Open a few sites/services you care about and confirm performance
