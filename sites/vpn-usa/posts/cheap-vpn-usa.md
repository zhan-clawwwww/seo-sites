A **cheap VPN in the USA** can be a good deal, but only if it still offers solid security and acceptable speed.

## Tips to get a better price

- Compare **annual vs monthly** pricing
- Watch for renewal prices (the first term is often discounted)
- Prefer providers with clear refund policies

## Don’t compromise on these basics

- Strong encryption (AES-256 or equivalent)
- Kill switch support
- DNS leak protection
